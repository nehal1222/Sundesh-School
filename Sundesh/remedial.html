Remedial Classes

Gyan Arjan Kendras or remedial classes are an initiative by SUNDESH to ensure that underprivileged students have access to quality education. Curated for students between 6 to 14 years of age, the classes addressed the special needs of weaker students and helped them improve and gain confidence. Our first Remedial Education Centre was operationalised in 2013 in Nidhawali village. This was soon extended to four centres. The classes are immensely popular as they allowed students to be more aware and ask questions in school when they do not understand a particular problem. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, all the centres were closed. Currently, one Remedial Education Centre in village Chauna has recommenced operations following all the necessary COVID-19 protocols. A total of 30 students from class 4 to class 8 are enrolled at the centre. And to date, 1,088 students have benefited from this initiative.
